Hi! The following PDF files are the 2021 & 2022 Design Reports of team DJS Skylark, of which I was a part of.

It was required to make a design report for each class of aircraft the team intended on participating. We, at DJS Skylark, participate in two classes of aicrafts: Micro Class & Regular Class. 

In 2021, the Regular Class aircraft secured a 1st worldwide rank and the Micro Class aircraft secured a 3rd worldwide rank. 

In 2022, the Micro Class aircraft secured the 1st rank and the Regular Class secured a 3rd worlwide rank.
